Why are we building

chikupos ?

World's fastest billing software for your restaurant and cafe

We started building chikupos because we saw a need for a restaurant management system that was simple to use, yet powerful enough to help streamline restaurant operations. Our goal is to make chikupos the go-to restaurant management system for small to medium sized restaurants.

We believe that by providing restaurants with an easy-to-use system, we can help them focus on what they do best - serving delicious food to their customers. With chikupos, restaurants can manage their inventory, track sales, and create custom reports - all from one central location.

What sets us apart from other restaurant management systems is our focus on simplicity and flexibility. We want to make sure that our system is easy to use, so that restaurant owners and managers can spend less time managing software, and more time running their business. Additionally, we offer a range of customization options, so that each restaurant can tailor the system to their specific needs.

If you're looking for a restaurant management system that will help you save time and streamline your operations, then chikupos is the right choice for you. Contact us today to learn more about our system, and how we can help you take your restaurant to the next level.
