Features & Benefits

Check out our list of awesome features below.

Smart Inventory

manage your inventory by keeping track of what is in stock, what needs to be ordered, and what needs to be restocked.

Crisp and Quick Billing

billing your customers is quick and easy. Our software allows you to create invoices and accept payments in a matter of seconds

Manage Multiple Outlet

Manage multiple outlets from one central location, saving time and frustration. You can grant access to your managers or franchise partners while still maintaining control over what level of access they have.

First Offline

If the internet goes down, this software will switch in offline mode and automatically sync data to the cloud when the internet is back up.

Cross Platform

You can run your software on any device, you can keep all your data in sync across devices, so you always have the most up-to-date information.

Consumptions

You can keep track of your ingredients and their consumption levels. This allows you to better control your costs and keep your menu fresh.
